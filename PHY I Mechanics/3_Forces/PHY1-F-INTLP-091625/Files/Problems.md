
### **Version 1**

**Situation:** A lamp hangs from a chain, which is attached to the ceiling.

* **Correct 1:** The downward pull (tension) by the lamp on the chain and the upward pull (tension) by the chain on the lamp.
* **Correct 2:** The downward pull (tension) by the chain on the ceiling and the upward pull (tension) by the ceiling on the chain.

* **Distractor 3:** The upward pull by the chain on the lamp and the downward pull by the chain on the ceiling.
